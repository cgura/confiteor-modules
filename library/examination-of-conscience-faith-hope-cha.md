# Faith
- **Category:** THEOLOGICAL_VIRTUES
- **Framework:** EXAMEN
- **Description:** Examining fidelity to God in faith—trusting Him, resisting temptations to doubt, and speaking/acting consistently with what you believe.

### 1
Did you meditate on the mysteries of the faith today?

> I meditated on the mysteries of the faith today.

### 2
Did you say a short act of faith today?

> I said a short act of faith today.

### 3
Did you pray today for an increase of faith?

> I prayed today for an increase of faith.

### 4
Did you tempt God by relying on your own strength rather than asking for His help?

> I tempted God by relying on my own strength rather than asking for His help.

### 5
Did you unnecessarily read or listen to things that belittle Catholic truths?

> I unnecessarily read or listened to things that belittled Catholic truths.

### 6
What did you do today to externally profess your faith?

> I professed my faith externally today.

### 7
Did human respect prevent you from expressing your faith?

> Human respect prevented me from expressing my faith.

### 8
Did you make serious efforts to resolve doubts or difficulties about the faith?

> I made serious efforts to resolve doubts or difficulties about the faith.

### 9
Did you defend the faith prudently and charitably?

> I defended the faith prudently and charitably.

### 10
Did you help someone overcome a difficulty against the faith?

> I helped someone overcome a difficulty against the faith.

# Hope
- **Category:** THEOLOGICAL_VIRTUES
- **Framework:** EXAMEN
- **Description:** Examining hope—how you responded when you were discouraged, how you trusted God’s providence, and whether you weakened hope through attachment or fear.

### 1
When you were discouraged, did you immediately say a short prayer?

> When I was discouraged, I immediately said a short prayer.

### 2
Did you say a short act of hope today?

> I said a short act of hope today.

### 3
Did you dwell on worries instead of dismissing them?

> I dwelled on worries instead of dismissing them.

### 4
Did you fail in hope by becoming overly attached to worldly things?

> I failed in hope by becoming overly attached to worldly things.

### 5
Did you recognize God’s providence in what happened today?

> I recognized God’s providence in what happened today.

### 6
Did you view your day from the perspective of eternity?

> I viewed my day from the perspective of eternity.

### 7
Did you keep confidence of salvation, relying on grace?

> I kept confidence of salvation, relying on grace.

### 8
Did you worry about the past in a way that weakened your confidence in mercy?

> I worried about the past in a way that weakened my confidence in mercy.

### 9
Did you combine deliberate action with even a momentary prayer for God’s help?

> I combined deliberate action with even a momentary prayer for God’s help.

### 10
How often did you complain (even internally)?

> I complained (even internally).

# Charity
- **Category:** THEOLOGICAL_VIRTUES
- **Framework:** EXAMEN
- **Description:** Examining charity—love of God and neighbor in words, thoughts, feelings, and practical choices.

### 1
Did you tell God that you love Him today?

> I told God that I love Him today.

### 2
Did you tell Jesus that you love Him with your whole heart today?

> I told Jesus that I love Him with my whole heart today.

### 3
Did you tell God that you love Him when you experienced something you dislike?

> I told God that I love Him when I experienced something I disliked.

### 4
Did you use your difficulties to tell God, “I love You,” through the trial?

> I used my difficulties to tell God, “I love You,” through the trial.

### 5
Did you see God’s love in allowing you to prove your love through crosses?

> I saw God’s love in allowing me to prove my love through crosses.

### 6
Did you see God’s grace in every person you met?

> I saw God’s grace in every person I met.

### 7
Did you fail in charity by speaking unkindly about others?

> I failed in charity by speaking unkindly about others.

### 8
Did you dwell on someone’s unkindness toward you?

> I dwelled on someone’s unkindness toward me.

### 9
Did you avoid someone whom you deliberately dislike?

> I avoided someone whom I deliberately disliked.

### 10
Did you try to keep conversation going with difficult people?

> I tried to keep conversation going with difficult people.

### 11
Were you stubborn in asserting your own will?

> I was stubborn in asserting my own will.

### 12
Did you show thoughtfulness by doing some small favor for someone?

> I showed thoughtfulness by doing some small favor for someone.

### 13
Did your mood prevent you from being thoughtful?

> My mood prevented me from being thoughtful.

### 14
Did you dwell on others’ weaknesses or faults?

> I dwelled on others’ weaknesses or faults.

### 15
Were you cheerful in dealing with others?

> I was cheerful in dealing with others.

### 16
Did you control uncharitable thoughts immediately?

> I did not control uncharitable thoughts immediately.

### 17
Did you pray for others today?

> I prayed for others today.

### 18
Did you write any letters today?

> I wrote letters today.

### 19
Did you control your emotions when irritated?

> I controlled my emotions when irritated.

### 20
Did you make any sacrifice for someone today?

> I made a sacrifice for someone today.
