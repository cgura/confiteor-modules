# Faith and Religion
- **Category:** FAITH_AND_RELIGION
- **Framework:** FAITH_AND_RELIGION

### 1
Have I denied or doubted God’s existence?

> I denied or doubted God’s existence.

### 2
Have I refused to believe God’s revelation?

> I refused to believe God’s revelation.

### 3
Have I believed in (or used) horoscopes, fortune telling, good luck charms, tarot cards, Ouija boards or reincarnation?

> I believed in (or used) horoscopes, fortune telling, good luck charms, tarot cards, Ouija boards or reincarnation.

### 4
Have I denied that I was a Catholic?

> I denied that I was a Catholic.

### 5
Have I abandoned the Catholic Faith for any period of time?

> I abandoned the Catholic Faith for a period of time.

### 6
Have I despaired of or presumed on God’s mercy?

> I despaired of or presumed on God’s mercy.

### 7
Have I neglected prayer for a long time?

> I neglected prayer for a long time.

### 8
Have I failed to pray daily?

> I failed to pray daily.

### 9
Have I blasphemed God or taken God’s Name in vain, cursed, or broken an oath or vow?

> I blasphemed God, took His Name in vain, cursed, or broke an oath or vow.

# Mass and Church Obligations
- **Category:** MASS_AND_CHURCH_OBLIGATIONS
- **Framework:** MASS_AND_CHURCH_OBLIGATIONS

### 1
Have I missed Mass on a Sunday or on a Holy Day of Obligation through my own fault?

> I missed Mass on a Sunday or Holy Day of Obligation through my own fault.

### 2
Am I always reverent in the presence of the Most Blessed Sacrament?

> I was not always reverent in the presence of the Most Blessed Sacrament.

### 3
Was I voluntarily inattentive at Mass?

> I was voluntarily inattentive at Mass.

### 4
Have I arrived at Mass late through my own fault?

> I arrived at Mass late through my own fault.

### 5
Have I left Mass early?

> I left Mass early.

### 6
Have I done unnecessary servile work on Sunday?

> I did unnecessary servile work on Sunday.

### 7
Have I failed to receive Holy Communion during the Easter Season?

> I failed to receive Holy Communion during the Easter Season.

### 8
Have I failed to confess my sins at least once a year?

> I failed to confess my sins at least once a year.

### 9
Have I received Holy Communion in the state of mortal sin?

> I received Holy Communion in the state of mortal sin.

### 10
Have I received Holy Communion without fasting for one hour or more from food and drink? (water and medicine are permitted)

> I received Holy Communion without fasting for one hour or more.

### 11
Have I made a bad Confession by deliberately not telling all the mortal sins I had committed?

> I made a bad Confession by deliberately withholding mortal sins.

### 12
Have I failed to contribute to the support of the Church?

> I failed to contribute to the support of the Church.

### 13
Have I eaten meat knowingly on the Fridays during Lent or on Ash Wednesday? (Ages 14+)

> I knowingly ate meat on Fridays during Lent or on Ash Wednesday.

### 14
Have I fasted as required on Ash Wednesday and Good Friday? (Ages 18-59)

> I failed to fast as required on Ash Wednesday and Good Friday.

# Personal Conduct and Relationships
- **Category:** PERSONAL_CONDUCT_AND_RELATIONSHIPS
- **Framework:** PERSONAL_CONDUCT_AND_RELATIONSHIPS

### 1
Have I disobeyed or disrespected my parents or legitimate superiors?

> I disobeyed or disrespected my parents or legitimate superiors.

### 2
Have I neglected my duties to my husband, wife, children, or parents?

> I neglected my duties to my husband, wife, children, or parents.

### 3
Have I failed to actively take an interest in the religious education and formation of my children?

> I failed to actively take an interest in the religious education and formation of my children.

### 4
Have I failed to educate myself concerning the teachings of the Church?

> I failed to educate myself concerning the teachings of the Church.

### 5
Have I given a full day’s work in return for a full day’s pay?

> I failed to give a full day’s work in return for a full day’s pay.

### 6
Have I given a fair wage to my employee(s)?

> I failed to give a fair wage to my employee(s).

### 7
Have I given scandal by what I said or did, especially to the young?

> I gave scandal by what I said or did, especially to the young.

### 8
Have I contributed to anyone’s abandoning of the Catholic Faith?

> I contributed to someone abandoning the Catholic Faith.

### 9
Was I impatient, angry, envious, unkind, proud, jealous, revengeful, hateful toward others, or lazy?

> I was impatient, angry, envious, unkind, proud, jealous, revengeful, hateful toward others, or lazy.

### 10
Have I given bad example, abused drugs, drank alcohol to excess, fought, or quarreled?

> I gave bad example, abused drugs, drank alcohol to excess, fought, or quarreled.

### 11
Have I physically injured or killed anyone?

> I physically injured or killed someone.

### 12
Have I had an abortion, or advised or supported an abortion?

> I had an abortion, or advised or supported an abortion.

### 13
Have I participated in or approved of the grave evil known as “mercy killing,” euthanasia, or doctor-assisted suicide?

> I participated in or approved of euthanasia or doctor-assisted suicide.

### 14
Have I attempted suicide or physically harmed myself?

> I attempted suicide or physically harmed myself.

### 15
Was I uncharitable in thought, word, or deed?

> I was uncharitable in thought, word, or deed.

### 16
Have I gossiped or revealed the faults or sins of others? (detraction)

> I gossiped or revealed the faults or sins of others.

### 17
Have I failed to keep secrets that I should have kept?

> I failed to keep secrets that I should have kept.

### 18
Have I forgiven those who have hurt or harmed me or my loved ones?

> I failed to forgive those who hurt or harmed me or my loved ones.

# Honesty and Integrity
- **Category:** HONESTY_AND_INTEGRITY
- **Framework:** HONESTY_AND_INTEGRITY

### 1
Have I stolen, cheated, helped, or encouraged others to steal, cheat, or keep stolen goods?

> I stole, cheated, helped, or encouraged others to steal, cheat, or keep stolen goods.

### 2
Have I made restitution for stolen goods?

> I failed to make restitution for stolen goods.

### 3
Have I deliberately failed to fulfill my contracts or to pay my bills?

> I deliberately failed to fulfill my contracts or to pay my bills.

### 4
Have I given or accepted bribes?

> I gave or accepted bribes.

### 5
Have I rashly gambled or speculated or deprived my family of the necessities of life?

> I rashly gambled or speculated or deprived my family of necessities.

### 6
Have I told lies? Deliberately in order to deceive or injure others? (slander or calumny)

> I told lies to deceive or injure others.

### 7
Have I committed perjury?

> I committed perjury.

### 8
Have I voted in accordance with a properly informed conscience, in a way consistent with the teachings of the Church, in regard to the sanctity of marriage and of human life issues?

> I failed to vote in accordance with a properly informed conscience.
